Biography

A native of Virginia, Chris grew up playing in rock and pop bands in Virginia and North Carolina before heading off to attend Berklee College of Music in Boston. After graduating Magna Cum Laude with a degree in music performance from Berklee, Chris served for four years in the U.S. Air Force Band as guitarist and staff arranger. In 1998, Chris moved to Seattle and quickly established himself as an important member of the area’s vibrant music scene.
Guitar Artistry

Chris is an extremely versatile performer, equally at home playing rock, funk, blues, R&B, gospel, country, and classical music. Chris maintains a busy schedule playing clubs, weddings and private parties, playing everything from solo guitar to classic jazz to rollicking R&B. In addition, Chris is a good sight reader, is comfortable in many styles and can fit into almost any musical situation.
